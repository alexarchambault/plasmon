```scala
object AtomicBoolean: java.util.concurrent.atomic
```

***

A `boolean` value that may be updated atomically. See the
[VarHandle](VarHandle) specification for descriptions of the properties
of atomic accesses. An `AtomicBoolean` is used in
applications such as atomically updated flags, and cannot be used
as a replacement for a [java.lang.Boolean](java.lang.Boolean).
